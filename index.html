About Us

Welcome to Machdubit Tech

We Are A Software Development Company & We Love Solving Business Problems.

Machdubit Tech is a software development and web development company based in Zimbabwe. Headquartered in Harare, Masvingo, Bulawayo, we are providing IT solutions and IT-enabled services. Ever since our establishment, we are growing every day to meet the growing demands of our clients and surpass their expectations. Our expertise in providing industry-specific solutions and custom services has enabled us to deliver absolute business solutions to our worldwide clients. We are providing our services to small as well as big corporate houses including individual entrepreneurs using our unmatched expertise and cutting edge technology..

Industries

We serve a large customer base across several industries and this has allowed us to gain an understanding of the wide variety of software needs and challenges that companies face today. Below are examples of some of the industries for which we have developed advanced software platforms and mobile apps, or integrated systems.

Property management

Our unique, easy-to-use property management software helps you to take control of your business. Powerful tools provide real-time insights, enabling you to operate more efficiently and communicate clearly with your residents - if you use a property management app from Machdubit.

Education and training

The education industry is going through many radical changes. It is becoming increasingly competitive. The educational organisations that want to maintain their competitive edge must keep pace with the technological changes taking place in the modern world in order engage effectively with their stakeholders. At Machdubit we supply our educational industry clients with software solutions that provide them with everything they need to achieve their goals in the most efficient way.

Staffing and recruitment

Our recruitment management software is a powerful and comprehensive web-based solution, designed for the temporary job sector. It speeds up the recruitment process and helps you to find the best available talent faster than ever.

Manufacturing and logistics

Our intuitive manufacturing software gives you full control: Equip your business with a powerful solution to manage orders and gain deep insights into your operations. Our advanced management and business intelligence tools will let you automate multiple processes, improving your efficiency and increasing profitability.


Healthcare and insurance

Healthcare is one of the most important and rapidly growing industries in the world’s economy. Whilst companies in this industry thrive, they consistently face budgetary, managerial and organisational challenges. Healthcare software development has an important role to play in terms of achieving better quality both in patient care and costs management. Facilities need advanced, integrated systems which support patient care, service delivery, settlements with financial institutions, or staff work and appointment management. On the other hand, modern technologies allow for the use of mobile applications in supporting a healthy lifestyle. Each of the above solutions requires good design, professional implementation and reliable support services
